Privacy Policy

How we protect the magic and your information.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Fairy Tale Child Care, we understand that the privacy of your family is of the utmost importance. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide to us through our website and during the enrollment process.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as:

  • Parent/Guardian names and contact information (email, phone, address).
  • Child's name, age, and developmental needs.
  • Health and emergency contact information required for licensing and safety.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for:

  • Communicating with parents regarding their child's care and school activities.
  • Ensuring the safety and well-being of all children in our care.
  • Managing enrollment and administrative requirements.
  • Complying with California Department of Social Services (CDSS) licensing regulations.

Protection of Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

Photos and Social Media

We only post photos of children on our website or social media with explicit written consent from the parent or guardian. Protecting our "little stars" is our primary concern.
