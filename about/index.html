Our Story

A magical place where happy childhood memories begin.

Nurturing Young Hearts & Minds

At Fairy Tale Child Care, we believe every child deserves a magical start to their journey. Our residential daycare in Palmdale was founded on the vision of creating a space that combines the warmth of a home with the excitement of a storybook adventure, providing a nurturing environment where children grow physically, mentally, socially, and emotionally.

Our facility is designed with child health as a top priority. We maintain a 100% Smoke Free and Pet Free environment, ensuring a safe and clean space for every little explorer.

The heart of our program is our beautiful backyard, featuring a premium playground with professional-grade swings, slides, and safe play zones. With a focus on safety, full-day nutrition (Breakfast, Snack, Lunch & Dinner), and personalized attention, we inspire children to create their own fairy tales every day.
