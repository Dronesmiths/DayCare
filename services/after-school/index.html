After-School Care

Productive Afternoons in a Supportive Environment

Where School Days End and Success Continues

For school-aged children (ages 6-10), the hours after school are a vital time for both productivity and relaxation. Fairy Tale Child Care provides a structured yet fun after-school environment where children can complete their schoolwork and enjoy their afternoons.

Our After-School Program Features:

  • Dedicated Homework Help: Our patient staff is available to assist children with their homework, ensuring they feel confident and prepared for the next school day.
  • Healthy & Magical Snacks: We provide nutritious snacks to keep energy levels up for afternoon activities.
  • Outdoor Recreation: Plenty of time for unstructured play in our safe, spacious backyard to burn off energy after a day in the classroom.
  • Creative Arts & Crafts: Regular opportunities to engage in building, painting, and imaginative projects.
  • Relaxed Atmosphere: A home-like setting where kids can unwind and socialize in a safe, supervised space.
  • Full Meal Service: We provide healthy Snacks and a nutritious Dinner for our after-school students.
  • 100% Smoke & Pet Free: A clean, healthy environment for productive afternoons.
