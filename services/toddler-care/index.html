Toddler Care

Adventure and Discovery Around Every Corner

Where Curiosity Becomes Knowledge

Toddlers are natural explorers, and at Fairy Tale Child Care, we provide the perfect environment for their endless curiosity. Our program for children aged 18 months to 3 years focuses on active discovery, social interaction, and the building blocks of independence.

Our Toddler Program Highlights:

  • Active Outdoor Play: Our beautiful, safe backyard playground features professional-grade equipment, including our magical castle-themed slide.
  • Social Skills Development: Through guided group play, toddlers learn to share, communicate, and build their first friendships.
  • Early Literacy & Language: Storytime, songs, and fingerplays are a daily staple, helping to expand vocabulary and a love for books.
  • Hands-On Discovery: Sensory bins, building blocks, and art projects allow toddlers to learn through touch and creativity.
  • Independence Building: We encourage toddlers to learn self-help skills like putting away toys and practicing table manners in a supportive way.

Safety is our top priority, and our Palmdale facility is fully optimized for active toddlers to roam and play with confidence and security.
