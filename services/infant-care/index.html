Infant Care

A Nurturing Fairy Tale Start for Your Little One

Personalized Care for Your Baby

At Fairy Tale Child Care, we understand that leaving your infant in someone else's care is a big step. Our infant program is designed to provide the same warmth, safety, and love they receive at home, within a professional and enriching environment.

Our Infant Program Includes:

  • Safe Sleep Standards: We follow rigorous safe sleep protocols with dedicated, clean cribs for every infant.
  • Sensory Exploration: Carefully selected, high-end sensory toys and activities to stimulate early development.
  • Individual Schedules: We honor each baby's unique eating and sleeping rhythm, ensuring they feel secure and settled.
  • Tummy Time & Movement: Daily opportunities for supervised physical discovery on soft, plush rugs in a bright, airy room.
  • Smoke & Pet Free: We maintain a 100% smoke and pet-free environment for your baby's health and safety.
  • Full Day Nutrition: We provide Breakfast, Snacks, Lunch, and Dinner fresh daily for growing infants.
  • Daily Reports: Detailed updates on feedings, diaper changes, and developmental milestones delivered to you every day.

Our small ratios allow our nurturing caregivers to form strong bonds with each infant, providing the consistent emotional support essential for healthy brain development.
