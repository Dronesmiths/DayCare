Preparing Little Hearts & Minds for a Lifetime of Success

Where Learning is a Fairy Tale

Our Preschool and Pre-K program is more than just daycare—it's a launchpad for lifelong learners. We utilize a unique storybook-themed curriculum that integrates core academic concepts into magical play-based scenarios.

Program Pillars:

  • Academic Readiness: We focus on early literacy, phonics, and mathematical thinking to ensure a smooth transition to Kindergarten.
  • Social-Emotional Learning: Small group settings allow us to help kids develop empathy, communication skills, and kindness.
  • Creative Discovery: From "Magic Science" experiments to "Knightly Arts & Crafts," we keep imagination at the center of learning.
  • World Exploration: We introduce children to geography, nature, and cultural diversity through engaging stories and projects.
  • 100% Smoke & Pet Free: We provide a pristine and healthy residential environment for all children.
  • Full Meal Service: Freshly prepared Breakfast, Snacks, Lunch, and Dinner served daily to support healthy growth.

Our goal is for every child to leave Fairy Tale Child Care with a love for learning and the confidence to succeed in a classroom environment.
