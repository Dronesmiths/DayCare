Summer Camp

Making Magical Memories Under the California Sun

A Summer of Enchantment & Discovery

When school is out, the magic begins! Fairy Tale Child Care's Summer Camp program offers children a vibrant and educational summer experience. We take full advantage of our beautiful shaded backyard garden to provide a season filled with laughter, learning, and outdoor fun.

Our Summer Camp Highlights:

  • Outdoor Arts & Crafts: From painting with watercolors to making nature-inspired mandalas, our garden becomes a studio for little artists.
  • Water Play Fridays: Safe and supervised water activities to keep cool and have a blast in the Palmdale sun.
  • Educational Adventures: Weekly themes that explore nature, science, and storytelling in engaging, hands-on ways.
  • Nature Exploration: Learning about local flowers, butterflies, and gardening in our beautiful backyard space.
  • Themed Celebrations: Magical picnics, "Knight and Princess" days, and special summer-themed events.

Our Summer Camp is designed to keep children active and engaged during the break, fostering new skills and creating friendships that last long after the season ends.
