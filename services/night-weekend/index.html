Night & Weekend Care

Safe Harbor for Families with Busy Schedules

Professional Care When You Need It Most

We know that the typical 9-to-5 doesn't work for every family in Palmdale. That's why Fairy Tale Child Care offers premium, professional care during evenings and weekends. Whether you work non-traditional hours or just need a safe place for your children, we are here for you.

Our Flexible Care Options:

  • Evening Care: A calm and structured environment for dinner, quiet play, and bedtime routines.
  • Weekend Availability: Engaging daytime activities and safe care on Saturdays and Sundays.
  • Peaceful Sleep Environment: Our nighttime setting features soft, magical lighting and clean, comfortable sleeping mats.
  • Nurturing Supervision: Constant, gentle supervision by qualified staff who specialize in non-traditional care hours.
  • Home-Like Comfort: Your child will feel safe and secure in our warm, residential facility, even when the sun goes down.

Our Night and Weekend program maintains the same high standards of safety and engagement as our daytime programs, ensuring your child receives the best care possible, regardless of the time.
